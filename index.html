BACHELOR's DEGREE PROGRAMS

SZABIST offers a four-year (eight semesters) BBA degree program of 144 credit hours. This is SZABIST's flagship program. For the BBA program, students are required to complete 46 courses and 03 credit hours Business Project completed.

MASTER's DEGREE PROGRAMS

SZABIST offers a two-year EMBA degree program for executives and middle level managers striving for excellence and greater challenges in their career. The unique program is speciﬁcally designed for those executives who aim to enhance their effciency and strategic thinking.

DOCTORAL's DEGREE PROGRAMS

SZABIST offers a PhD Degree in Computing that can be completed during the evenings in three years after the MS degree graduate. A total of 48 credit hours must be completed.The degree can be earned in minimum 3 years and maximum 8 years.
